Manav Blood Donation Movement

“NO HUMAN DIE FOR WANT OF BLOOD, A VITAL FLUID HAVING NO SUBTITUTE”

Since 1971 Tarun Mitra Mandal regularly educates and explains the importance of voluntary blood donation and motivates healthy people to donate blood. From 1971 to till May 2015 the organization has been instrumental in collecting more than 1,17,842 units of blood. At present, every year 3 camps of full day are arranged. Tarun Mitra Mandal is the leading organization for voluntary blood donation and for that matter is the only social organization of megapoly now having a track record of collecting an average of 3500 blood units in a single day camp.

The blood donor are honoured with (1) Blood Donor’s Credit Card, (2) Momento or Gift. As a result of regular tie up with local government hospital- K.E.M. (Parel) which has the most modern and the biggest plasma fractional units, the lives of 5 to 8 patients are caused to be saved out of the each of unit blood collected at blood donation camps.

Book Bank for needy students

The journey of TMM began with the managing Book Bank of Shri K.V.O.S. Jain Mahajan in 1969. At the beginning of each academic year, students get the entire set of required textbooks against a nominal refundable deposit.

Till June 2015 more than 34,000 students have been extended the helping hand to pursue their studies.

Incidental to this, notebooks, school uniforms, raincoats/umbrella and other education related things are distributed free of cost to the poorest of poor of the society irrespective of caste, creed, colour, faith or religion. Many students, with the support of this project, have fulfilled their ambitions of pursuing higher studies and are now well placed in the society, after graduating in streams like Medical, Engineering, Chartered Accountancy, etc.

Book Bank centers are operated at Chincpokli, Mulund, Ambernath, Nalasopala, Ghatkopar, Dombivili and Andheri so as to reach out to as many needy students as possible.

We issue fresh sets of textbooks at a refundable amount of Rs.100, and digests at a refundable amount of Rs.250. While the students return their set of books with minimum depreciation, the paid amount is refunded.

Every year around 1,300 students are issued with Text Books & Digests. In 2014 around 1,392 sets of books were issued.

Yuvraj Eye Donation Movement

“Donate Eye Spread Light”

Having carved a niche in the blood-donation movement and made a prime place for itself, Tarun Mitra Mandal Had decided to launch the Yuvraj Eye Donation Movement on the 50th anniversary of India’s Independence i.e 15th august,1997. Eye Donation Movement is inspired by Shri K.V.O.S. Jain Mahajan.

The Slogan coined was “Blindness-Quit India”.

Unfortunately, there is woeful lack of knowledge annual average Seventy Five thousand people die, than 25 lacs corneal blind people are awiting for restoration of sight and out of them more than 60% are children below the age 12. Tarun Mitra Mandal, has setup a human chain of more than 500 people comprising of voluntary devoted social workers, general medical practitioners and eye surgeons.
After death if family member wish to donate the eye of decrease please call on 1919 or Kusum VIra : 24922897

The entire geographical area of the city has been broadly divided into 3 region.

  1. Down town.
  2. Suburbs along Central Railway.
  3. Suburbs along the Western Railway.

These three regions are further divided into 37 zones each of such zone has a team consisting of 3 to 7 social workers and doctors. Infact by doing so, Tarun Mitra Mandal has set-up 37 mobile eye banks.

Within less than 14 years of its operation, i.e. on 1-5-2015 more than 14,194 eye balls are collected and given to the Eye Bank Co-ordination and Research Centre, which is affiliated to the International Federation of Eye and Tissue Banks, Baltimore (USA), for evaluation and processing and further distribution of corneas to the beneficiaries. Tarun Mitra Mandal has got a place of pride for having received accreditation from Internation Federation of Eye Banks an apex world that Yuraj Eye Donation Movement is largest city wide organization of the world.
